## Overview

Distribution systems encompass the infrastructure that supports network operations, including cable plant design, power systems, HVAC, and fire suppression.

- Structured cabling standards (TIA/EIA-568)
- Power redundancy and UPS sizing
- Environmental monitoring and cooling
- Fire suppression methods (wet pipe, dry pipe, pre-action, clean agent)
