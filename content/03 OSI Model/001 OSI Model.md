## 🧠 Main Takeaways
- **7 types of layers** **(PDNTSAP)**
	- reference model
	- we don't use this in our real world instead we use TCP/IP model
- now the 7 layers of OSI's model are :
	- **application** (DATA)
	- **presentation** (DATA)
	- **session** (DATA)
	- **transport** --> (SEGMENT)
	- **network** (PACKET)
	- **data link** (FRAMES)
	- **physical** (BITS)
