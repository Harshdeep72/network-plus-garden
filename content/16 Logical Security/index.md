## Overview

Logical security covers identity and access management, authentication methods, encryption, PKI, and digital certificates that protect data at rest and in transit.

## Key Concepts

- AAA framework (authentication, authorization, accounting)
- Symmetric vs asymmetric encryption
- PKI hierarchy and certificate trust chains
- IPSec transport vs tunnel mode
