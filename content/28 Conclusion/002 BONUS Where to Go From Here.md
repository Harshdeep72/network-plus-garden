## 🔑 Key Terminology
* **100% Pass Guarantee**: A policy offered by Dion Training where, if a student passes the course on their website but fails the corresponding certification exam, the company pays for the exam retake.
* **Take2 Option**: An add-on service for exam vouchers that allows a candidate to retake a certification exam at a reduced cost if they fail the first attempt.
* **Exam Voucher**: A unique code purchased to pay for and schedule a professional certification exam (e.g., CompTIA, ITIL, PRINCE2).
* **Regional Pricing**: A strategy used by certification bodies (like CompTIA) to adjust the cost of exams and vouchers based on the candidate's geographic location.

## 🔄 How It Works (Processes)
* **Accessing Best Pricing**: Students are directed to `diontraining.com/udemy` to access instructor coupon links, which ensure the lowest possible price for courses on the Udemy platform, bypassing dynamic pricing fluctuations.
* **Dion Training Website vs. Udemy**:
 * **Udemy**: Focuses on video training, quizzes, and practice exams.
 * **Dion Training Website**: Offers a comprehensive "study solution" including videos, quizzes, study guides, practice exams, integrated hands-on labs (40 hours of guided training), and the 100% Pass Guarantee.
* **Purchasing Vouchers**: Vouchers are purchased via the Dion Training online store. The process requires the user to input their location to ensure the voucher is valid for their specific region and priced according to local standards.
